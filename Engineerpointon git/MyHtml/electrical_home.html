    Electrical Safety

    Electrical safety is most important one must learn before working on any field. Every Electrician should follow given safety procedure of working.

    1. Disconnecting

      2. Before working on any Electrical machine make sure all the phases are disconnected and all circuit breakers are off.

    2. Resetting

      3. Once you have disconnected all circuit breakers and fuses make sure you put a safety lock. This will ensure your safety, if anyone else tries to connect those fuses.

    3. Determine the absence of voltage

      4. Ensure absence of voltage with a multimeter or any voltage measuring device. Each supply line must be tested against earth.

    4. Earthing and shortcircuiting

      5. In case of earthing and short-ciruiting, all the external conductors are interconnected to the operational earth by special connection lines. If the plant switched on unwarrentedly no dangerous situation will occure

    5. Insulation

      6. Covering protecting or insulatiog the system components under voltage will prevent the live parts from coming into direct contact with external bodies. Tha mats made of insulated materials are often used for covering the surrounding parts.
